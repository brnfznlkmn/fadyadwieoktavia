About Fadya

Born and raised in Bekasi, Fadya is a 22-year-old final-year Accounting student currently embarking on a journey as a content creator.

Fadya is passionate about finance, marketing strategies, and digital content creation. She actively manages various social media platforms to deliver informative and engaging content. Leveraging tools such as Canva, CapCut, and Adobe, Fadya channels her creativity into producing visually appealing and impactful content.

As a final-year student, Fadya’s academic focus lies in financial statement analysis and risk management. She is also eager to explore digital business strategies and how social media can be utilized to drive success in businesses. Committed to continuous learning and adapting to emerging trends, Fadya is dedicated to growing both professionally and personally through hands-on experience in the real world.
